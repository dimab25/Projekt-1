Accomondation・宿泊施設

The chepest way to stay over night in Tokyo is to sleep in a Capsule Hotel, Manga Cafe, Internetcafe or a Love Hotel. Very convinient, when you missed the last train.

Capsule hotels in Japan are like sleeping in a futuristic sock drawer—tiny, tidy, and surprisingly cozy. Perfect for budget travelers or anyone curious about living like a space-age sardine!

Internet cafes in Japan are like gamer paradise meets survival bunker—cozy cubicles with Wi-Fi, unlimited drinks, and even showers. Some people check in for emails; others basically move in!
